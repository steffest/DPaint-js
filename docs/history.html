History

Version 0.2.0

2026-02-16
A cumulation of 2 years of bug fixes and optimizations.

Most notable changes

  • Animation support (including GIF and IFF ANIM import/export)
  • Pressure sensitivity and multitouch improvements (pen-only support)
  • Texture brushes
  • Instant Palette Lock
  • 12 and 9 bit color depths
  • Resizable panels
  • Arc tool
  • Gallery
  • New filters: Texture and Dehaze
  • Enhanced Save dialog with save-options
  • Recorder: record your drawing process and export timelapse videos
  • Layers to frames / frames to layers
  • Invert selection
  • Better documentation
  • Export to HAM6, HAM8 and SHAM (thanks Rob Coenen!)

Amiga Specific

  • 12 bit color depth
  • Color Cycle with IFF export
  • Reading and writing IFF ANIM files
  • Reading and writing of HAM images (HAM6, HAM8 and SHAM, thanks Rob Coenen!)

Atar Specific

  • Better handling of Deluxe Paint for Atari formats (Byterun2 compression, thanks Nicolas Ramz)
And last but not least: Dpaint.js has moved to a new domain: dpaint.app.
